Our Story:

I imagine our story is quite similar to many other restaurateurs. It all started with a little Food Network binge watching. Hours later we were convinced we too could be part of the top barbecue restaurants in the country, and we started working on our house seasonings immediately. Many many hours later we finally picked our favorite recipes and decided it was time to open up shop!!

In today's world we realize it's more important than ever to provide top-of-the-line products, so we have spent a lot of time finding high-quality farmers to buy our meat from. All of our recipes are made with organic, pasture-raised pork and chicken. This is part of our secret to providing the best flavor this side of the Mississippi. Whether you're in town on business, pleasure, or you've lived here your whole life - you haven't tasted grade A barbecue until you've been to HogTank. Stop by and meet us in person!
